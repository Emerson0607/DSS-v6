personUser's Account Information

To change your profile information:

  • 1. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  • 2. Select "Edit Profile" from the dropdown menu.
  • 3. Update your profile details as needed.
  • 4. Click "Save" to apply the changes.

To change your password:

  • 1. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  • 2. Select "Change Password" from the dropdown menu.
  • 3. Enter your current password and then your new password.
  • 4. Click "Save" to apply the changes.

If you forgot your password:

  • 1. Visit the login page.
  • 2. Click on the "Forgot Password?" link.
  • 3. Enter your email address.
  • 4. Follow the instructions sent to your email to reset your password.
